Reinforce Your Value with a Green Belt

Bridge the gap between Lean Six Sigma theory and practical application by earning a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt certificate 100% online from Emory Continuing Education. During the six-week course, you will gain the Lean Six Sigma knowledge and skills to help make processes, products, and services faster and more efficient.

You’ll also learn the differences between Lean, Six Sigma and Lean Six Sigma, the roles and responsibilities of Lean Six Sigma project teams, and how to successfully apply key tools and techniques outside of the classroom.

Program Outcomes

Upon your successful completion of the Lean Six Sigma Green Belt course, you will be able to:

  • Identify and charter potential Lean Six Sigma projects consistent with organizational needs
  • Assist with data collection and analysis for Black Belt projects
  • Execute a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt project through all five DMAIC phases (define, measure, analyze, improve, control)
  • Assess appropriate Lean Six Sigma tools during the execution of a Green Belt project (i.e., be able to apply tools to your unique situation)
  • Employ software programs (Minitab and/or MS Excel) to analyze data to verify and validate root cause and associated solutions
  • Demonstrate the knowledge required to successfully pass the Lean Six Sigma Green Belt certification examination offered through ECE at no additional cost.
